FitFam App Guide

Welcome to FitFam, your ultimate fitness companion! With FitFam, you can create custom workouts tailored to your fitness goals. Follow these steps to get started:

Step 1: Sign Up or Log In

If you're a new user, click on the "Sign Up" button to create an account. If you already have an account, click on "Log In" to access your existing account.

Step 2: Explore the Exercise Catalog

Once you're logged in, navigate to the Exercise Catalog section. Here, you'll find a vast collection of exercises to choose from. Use the filtering options to narrow down the exercises based on your preferences.

Step 3: Customize Your Workouts

Select exercises from the catalog that align with your fitness goals. You can add them to your customized workout plan by clicking on the "Add to Workout" button next to each exercise.

Step 4: View and Edit Your Workouts

In the My Workouts section, you can view and manage your customized workout plans. Edit the exercises, reorder them, or remove exercises as needed to create the perfect workout routine for yourself.

Step 5: Track Your Progress

Keep track of your progress by logging your workout sessions and recording sets, reps, and weights used. The app will help you monitor your progress over time and stay motivated.

Step 6: Stay Consistent and Stay Fit

Consistency is key! Stick to your workout routine, challenge yourself, and keep pushing towards your fitness goals. FitFam is here to support you every step of the way.
